    • Talent Acquisition

    Every organization operates within a context that shapes its identity, actions, and public image.

    The success of a company largely depends on having the right people to achieve its goals and meet expectations.

    From my perspective, I offer three types of consulting and services to help you build the best team for your organization.

    To achieve this, we will first define together the requirements for each position, which includes: an analysis of technical skills, interpersonal abilities, and alignment with the company's cultural values.

    - Internal recruitment:Identifying potential candidates within your organization who can fill key positions, providing support throughout the entire process.

    - External recruitment: Searching for candidates in the job market, with expertise in IT, product, audit, finance-accounting, and management roles.

    - Hybrid recruitment: A combined approach that includes internal talent analysis and external search if needed, optimizing rotations and new hires.
