    About me

    Hi there! I am Maca. I have been working on a People team for over 7 years. I’ve graduated with a Bachelor's Degree in HR Management. Since then, I have been focusing on my main mission, which is to create opportunities to connect Talent, put in my grain of sand into People’s lives, develop their potential, truly enjoy what they do, and produce an overall positive impact on society and the environment. I specialized in talent acquisition, change management and labor psychopedagogy. Currently, I’m working as a People Partner, at a non-profit organization that seeks to accelerate the transition to clean energy in emerging and developing countries. Furthermore, during the last few months I have been training as a vocational coach and career guidance.
