    • Vocational orientation

    The focus of this service is to guide you through the process of self-discovery and self-awareness regarding your life project. This involves identifying your personal strengths, interests, skills, and cultural values, as well as understanding the ever-changing demands of the job market.

    The process includes the following stages:

    - Initial Assessment: A session to understand your concerns, goals, potential and personal context.

    - Self-Discovery: Use of tools such as vocational tests, interactive exercises, and skills analysis.

    - Job Market Exploration: Research on industry trends, in-demand roles, and growing sectors.

    - Vocational Plan Design: Development of a personalized plan with clear goals and specific actions.

    - Follow-up: Ongoing support to track progress, address questions, and adjust the strategy as needed.
